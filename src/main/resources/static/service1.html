Tasty, Juicy and Sweet Organic Mangoes...

Our organic mangoes are grown without chemicals, using natural compost and traditional farming methods. Handpicked at peak ripeness, they’re juicy, sweet, and full of authentic flavor—just as nature intended. Every mango you receive is a testament to our commitment to purity, sustainability, and the rich heritage of Indian farming.

On our organic farm, we rely on time-tested practices that respect nature. Instead of synthetic fertilizers or chemical pesticides, we use organic compost, cow dung manure, neem-based sprays, and other eco-friendly inputs. These natural methods not only protect the environment but also enrich the soil, allowing our mango trees to grow stronger and bear fruit that’s healthier, tastier, and more nutrient-rich.

We proudly cultivate a wide range of regional and seasonal mango varieties, each with its own distinct charm. Our orchard includes the famed Banganapalli, the royal Alphonso, the juicy Imam Pasand, and the tangy-sweet Totapuri. In addition to these popular varieties, we also nurture native treasures like Senduram, known for its vibrant orange hue and sweetness, and Kesar, with its rich saffron flavor and smooth texture.

For those who love traditional South Indian tastes, we offer Neelisha, a small-sized variety cherished for its fragrance and juiciness; Kili Mukku, a slightly tangy, fiberless mango ideal for pickles and raw consumption; and Chakkara Kutty, a delightful mini mango with honeyed notes perfect for snacking and small servings. These varieties represent not just fruit—but the culture and climate of the regions they come from.

Every mango is allowed to ripen naturally on the tree and is carefully handpicked at the right moment. We never use artificial ripening agents like carbide. Once harvested, the fruits are gently cleaned, sorted, and packed at the farm itself—preserving their freshness and flavor while ensuring zero chemical contamination.

By choosing our organic mangoes, you are not only tasting the authentic sweetness of nature but also supporting local farmers, clean agriculture, and a more sustainable planet. Whether you’re enjoying them fresh, in juices, desserts, or pickles, our mangoes promise a pure and wholesome experience in every bite.
