Bursting with Flavor, Rooted In Nature....

Our organic guavas are grown using natural compost and eco-friendly practices. Rich in flavor and nutrients, they’re handpicked at peak ripeness to ensure freshness, taste, and purity in every bite. Grown in the warm tropical climate of South India, these guavas are a true reflection of clean, sustainable farming.

We cultivate our guavas without using chemical fertilizers or synthetic pesticides. Instead, we rely on organic compost, cow dung manure, and traditional herbal sprays made from neem, ginger, and other natural ingredients. This keeps the soil fertile, supports biodiversity, and helps the fruits develop their natural sweetness and aroma.

We grow native South Indian varieties such as Perakka, Arka Mridula, and Local Pink Guava, which are known for their soft flesh, juicy texture, and mildly sweet to tangy taste. These fruits are packed with Vitamin C, antioxidants, and dietary fiber—making them a delicious and nutritious choice for your daily diet.

Each guava is allowed to ripen naturally on the tree and is harvested by hand at the perfect stage. After harvesting, they are cleaned and sorted without any artificial ripening, wax, or preservatives. The result is a guava that’s as fresh and wholesome as nature intended.

Whether eaten raw, blended into smoothies, or added to chutneys and salads, our guavas offer a burst of tropical flavor and natural goodness. By choosing our organic guavas, you're supporting eco-conscious farming and treating yourself to clean, healthy produce.

Pure, flavorful, and organically grown—our South Indian guavas are a perfect balance of taste and health.
